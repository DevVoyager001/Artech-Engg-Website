About Artech Engineering India

Artech Engineering India is a renowned specialist in steel structural fabrication and erection, piping, project, and mechanical maintenance work, as well as civil jobs. With a rich history spanning over 35 years, our company has a legacy of excellence and a proven track record of delivering high-quality services to the steel, power, and chemical industries.

Our Journey

Our journey began as Suraj Construction, collaborating with Ispat Profiles Pune in 1985. Over the years, we continued to grow and evolve. In 1993, we became a part of Ispat Industries Ltd., Dolvi, and in 2010, we rebranded ourselves as Artech Engineering India.
